Get Indonesia Electronic Customs Declaration Today

All passengers traveling to Indonesia must complete a Indonesia Electronic Customs Declaration. This is a mandatory requirement to go through customs clearance.

The e-CD form for Indonesia requires travelers to declare any goods they will bring into the country. This then decides which customs channel the passenger should pass through, and helps Indonesian customs to carry out any required checks.

The Indonesia Electronic Customs Declaration is easy to obtain. You can complete the form before you depart using the simple online system.
